How Do You Know You've Found a Zombie?


Use this handy checklist to determine if your new friend is a zombie or not.


Is your new friend trying to convince you to let them eat your brains? □ Yes □ No
Has your new friend recently died and come back to life? □ Yes □ No
Does your new friend have fresh or decaying torn limbs? □ Yes □ No
Can you see the spinal column of your new friend through the wound where their lower body used to be? □ Yes □ No
Does your new friend still move after putting a stake through their heart? □ Yes □ No
...what about a machete? □ Yes □ No
Is your friend trying to convince you to Twilight was the best movie *ever*? □ Yes □ No

If you answered "Yes" to any of these questions, your new friend is definitely a zombie.
